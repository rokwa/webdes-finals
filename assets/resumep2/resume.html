Personal Information

Get to know me better :)


Hi there! Call me Lou.

As a kid, I loved art, all forms of it. I've drawn on every paper I got, even on walls too. Then came in the digital age, where I was introduced to computers and discovered digital art. And I went crazy with it. I remember being so fascinated being able to create art without actually using pencils, imagine how much more amazed I was when I knew about printers, I printed everything I could print. Growing up, I maintained the same hobby, creating art and being fascinated by computers, slowly but surely, I've grown to know what I wanted to be in the future. I'm still learning, but I'll get there. My love for art and computers is just as big as it was before, growing, even.
